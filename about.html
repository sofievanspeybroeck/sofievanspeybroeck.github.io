get to know us!

When was The Cupcake Factory founded?

The Cupcake Factory was founded in 2017, when I decided to take matters into my own hands.
I wanted to do something with my life-long passion: baking cupcakes!

Is it possible to book the cupcake factory all year round?

Yes, indeed! We are always looking for new opportunities and cool events to sell our cupcakes! On our contact page, you can find all the information to get in contact with our team!

How much does the service cost?

We can't quite say, it all depends on the size of your event and the amount of people attending. You can always contact us for more information!
